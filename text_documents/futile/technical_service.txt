DOCUMENT: TECHNICAL SERVICE BULLETIN - BRAKING SYSTEMS (REF: TSB-2026-04)
SUBJECT: HYBRID BRAKE PAD REPLACEMENT AND SENSOR CALIBRATION

PROCEDURE A: REMOVAL
1. Lift vehicle and remove all four wheels. 
2. Disconnect the electronic wear sensor harness (Note: do not pull by the wires; use the plastic clip release).
3. Retract the electronic parking brake (EPB) using the diagnostic tablet interface before compressing the caliper.

PROCEDURE B: CALIBRATION
Once new pads are installed, the technician must perform a 'Soft Reset' on the ECU.
1. Turn ignition to ON (Position II).
2. Press and hold the Brake Pedal and Accelerator simultaneously for 15 seconds.
3. The dashboard should display 'BRAKE SERVICE MODE: COMPLETE'.

WARNING: Failure to reset the ECU after pad replacement will result in a 'Brake Malfunction' warning light and may disable the regenerative braking system.
